V2 · Roadmap layout exploration

Four layouts for the Process section.

Same three phases, same copy, same focus areas. Four different ways to arrange them on a single page so the section scans quickly and wastes less horizontal space. Scroll through, then tell me which direction to commit to in the main V2 mockup.

V1 · Compact Tile V2 · Sidebar Phase V3 · Timeline Rows V4 · Narrowed Cards
V1Compact Tile

Your suggestion. Phase head stays as a full-width black bar across the top of each card. Below it, a 2-column split: body paragraph on the left, focus areas stacked above outcome on the right. The outcome stops being a full-width footer and becomes part of the right column. Each card reads top-down without the body ever running to an unreadable width.

Process

How We Modernize You

01

Diagnostic Sprint

Analysis before assembly

Every partnership begins with a time-capped audit of your growth and operations stack. This sprint identifies where work is leaking — human effort, missed signals, stalled handoffs — and locates your highest-margin opportunities for automation.

Focus Areas
  • Workflow and process efficiency
  • Data and measurement maturity
  • Automation opportunity ranking
  • Customer and operational lifecycle friction
  • AI and agent readiness
Outcome A clear, prioritized view of where change will drive the highest return.
02

Instrumentation

Clean data. Clear workflows.

Agentic systems are only as reliable as the substrate beneath them. We get your data, processes, and tooling to a state where agents can run on top of them without breaking. Unified sources of truth, documented workflows, clear rules of engagement — the unglamorous work that makes automation actually work.

Focus Areas
  • Unified data & measurement infrastructure
  • Workflow definition and documentation
  • Governance, guardrails, and eval criteria
  • Automation-readiness across systems
Outcome Your data, processes, and governance are automation-ready.
03

Autonomous Orchestration

Scale independent of headcount

Finally, we transition your team from manual execution to system oversight. We design the agentic workflows that manage day-to-day decisions and actions in real time — across acquisition, operations, customer success, and revenue. This builds a self-optimizing engine that improves your bottom line through intelligent scale.

Focus Areas
  • Agentic workflow orchestration
  • Automated decisioning (budget, routing, prioritization)
  • Predictive operational & CRM orchestration
  • Real-time measurement and feedback loops
Outcome A self-optimizing operating model that scales predictably while reclaiming up to 40% of your team’s strategic capacity.
V2Sidebar Phase

Each phase card has a narrow left sidebar — a vertical tab — carrying the big phase number plus title/sub. The rest of the card is a content area: body paragraph on top, then a focus-areas + outcome 2-col below. The sidebar treats each phase like a numbered chapter opener. Denser than V1, stronger phase identity.

V3Timeline Rows

Drops the card chrome entirely. Each phase is a row with three columns: phase identity on the left (big mono number, moss rule, title, sub), body paragraph in the middle, focus areas + outcome on the right. Thin rules separate rows. Feels like a changelog or a printed brief — the most information-dense, least decorated option.

V4Narrowed Cards

Each phase card is constrained to ~860px and centered on the page. Content stacks vertically inside: phase head strip, body paragraph, focus areas, then outcome as a tinted footer strip. No side-by-side columns inside the card — everything flows downward. The narrower width forces a readable column naturally. Feels like a chapter page in a printed pitch document.
